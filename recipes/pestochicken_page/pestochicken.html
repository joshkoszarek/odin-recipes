Pesto Chicken

Description

This simple pesto chicken meal is a great solution for dinner and is ready in 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, pounded to an even thickness
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup shredded parmesan
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Spray a 9x13 baking dish with non-stick spray.
  2. Sprinkle the chicken with salt and pepper and then coat each piece in the pesto sauce.
  3. Place the chicken in the prepared baking dish and top with any leftover pesto. Sprinkle on the mozzarella and parmesan and top with halved tomatoes.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees.
  5. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with basil before serving.
